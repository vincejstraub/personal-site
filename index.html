I am an interdisciplinary researcher and multimedia artist currently working at the Alan Turing Institute in the Public Policy Programme led by Helen Margetts. I am interested in understanding the development of collective intelligence for addressing sustainability challenges.

I have been fortunate to work with exceptional researchers in the past, spanning the fields of sociology, animal behavior, digital ethics, and computer science. After undergraduate studies at King's College London, I interned at the Nesta Centre for Collective Intelligence Design where I worked with Geoff Mulgan. For graduate studies, I studied at the Oxford Internet Institute where I was advised by Taha Yasseri and worked with Mariarosaria Taddeo in the Digital Ethics Lab. Prior to the Turing I supported a project led by Max Wolf in the Science of Intelligence joint Cluster of Excellence

Alongside research, my works in video and other digital media have been exhibited at galleries including Tate Modern and the Stedelijk Museum. Public engagement is also a key part of my working practice; I have previously been an ambassador for the Jubel European Democracy Festival and a member of the standing committee for the European Forum Alpbach.
